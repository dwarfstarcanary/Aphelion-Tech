Mission Statement

Aphelion Tech believes that every gamer should be able to traverse the game world in their own unique space. For every personality and every wallet, Aphelion Tech makes your dream gaming setup possible.

At Aphelion Tech, we believe that each gamer should be able to traverse the game world in their own unique orbit. Aphelion Tech offers customized PC builds and setups tailored to the preferred style and budget of the client. We believe that the equipment you use and the environment you are in directly affects your gaming experience. Aphelion Tech aims to combine art and technology so that each user can make their gaming environment or workspace uniquely theirs! Aphelion Tech believes that beauty doesn’t have to break the bank and offers affordable options for all components, from budget to premium.

Unlike our competitors, we offer total personalization for each client via a simple to use database. Through this database, the customer can create their very own PC model by selecting the modifications. The modifications include custom colors, lighting, and graphics for each component. If there is something specific that a client wants, they can schedule a consultation with a designer who will work to make a mockup for the client.

Individual custom parts are also available for sale for those who aren’t looking for an entire build. Aside from the actual PC itself, clients have the option to continue to customize their setup with matching accessories such as keyboards, mice, mouse pads, and headsets. Aphelion Tech prides itself on quality service and offers a free year of tech support and warranty with the option for add-ons.
